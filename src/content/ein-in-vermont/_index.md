### Federal EIN vs Vermont State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Vermont state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Vermont State Tax ID** — From Department of Taxes, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
