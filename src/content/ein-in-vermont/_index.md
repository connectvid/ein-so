## Is a Vermont EIN Different From a <Accent>Federal EIN?</Accent>

A Vermont EIN is not different from a federal EIN, because there is only one EIN and it is federal. The IRS issues the same 9-digit number to a Vermont business that it issues to a business in any other state. Vermont does not run its own EIN system.

Vermont businesses often confuse two separate identifiers. The federal EIN comes from the IRS and covers federal tax filings. The Vermont state tax account comes from the Vermont Department of Taxes and covers state-level obligations. You may need both.

| Identifier | Issued By | Used For | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal taxes, banking, Form SS-4 | $0 |
| Vermont state tax account | Vermont Department of Taxes | State income, sales, withholding tax | Varies |

Your EIN does not replace Vermont state tax registration. If your business hires employees in Vermont, sells taxable goods, or owes Vermont income tax, you register separately with the Department of Taxes. See our [state tax ID guide](/state-tax-id/) for the distinction. Confirm your specific Vermont registration duties with a CPA, because ein.so does not provide tax advice.

## How Do You Get an EIN <Accent>in Vermont?</Accent>

You get an EIN in Vermont by filing IRS Form SS-4 through one of four methods. The process is identical in every state, because the EIN is federal. Non-residents without an SSN use the fax method, since the online tool is blocked for them.

| Method | Speed | Cost | SSN Required? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Online (irs.gov) | Instant | Free | Yes |
| Fax (855-215-1627) | 4-7 business days | Free / $49 | No |
| Phone (international applicants) | Same day | Free | No |
| Mail | 4-6 weeks | Free | No |

The online method is the fastest, but it requires a Social Security Number, so non-US residents cannot use it. The fax method is the standard path for non-residents and takes 4-7 business days when the form is error-free. See [how to get an EIN](/how-to-get-ein/) for the complete walkthrough and [EIN processing time](/ein-processing-time/) for current timelines.

### After You Receive Your EIN in Vermont

1. Register with the Vermont Department of Taxes for any state taxes you owe
2. Obtain local Vermont business licenses if your activity requires them
3. [File your BOI report](/boi-filing/) with FinCEN (a federal requirement)
4. Open a [US business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) with your EIN

## How Does a Non-Resident Get a Vermont LLC <Accent>EIN Without an SSN?</Accent>

A non-resident gets a Vermont LLC EIN by filing Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627 and entering a passport number on Line 7b instead of an SSN. No SSN, ITIN, or US visit is required. The IRS issues the EIN regardless of the applicant's country of residence.

Many Vermont LLCs are owned by non-US residents who want a US business identity, US banking, and access to US payment processors. The EIN is the first step. Here is how the SS-4 maps to a non-resident applicant.

<Step step="1" title="Form Your Vermont LLC">Register your LLC with the Vermont Secretary of State, or choose another state through a registered agent. You can complete formation online from abroad. This gives you a legal entity and address to attach the EIN to.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your valid passport, your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your foreign mailing address. No SSN, ITIN, or US residency is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full legal name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### SS-4 Field Guide for Non-Resident Vermont Applicants

| SS-4 Field | Non-Resident Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Mailing address) | Your foreign address |
