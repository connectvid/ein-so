<Capsule>**Podcasters need an EIN if they operate as an LLC, corporation, or hire employees.** Podcast businesses earning from sponsorships, ads, or Patreon need an EIN for proper tax reporting. The EIN is free from the IRS. Sole proprietors can use their SSN but an EIN is recommended for separating business and personal finances. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

## Do Podcasters <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Podcasters LLC | Yes (required) |

| Hobby/side income only | No |

### Why Podcasters Get an EIN

1. **Tax compliance** — Required on business tax returns
2. **Business banking** — Open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
3. **Privacy** — Protect your SSN on invoices and W-9s
4. **Credibility** — Professional business identity
5. **Hiring** — Required for [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/)
