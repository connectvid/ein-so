<Capsule>**Change your EIN address by filing IRS Form 8822-B or sending a signed letter to the IRS at Cincinnati, OH 45999.** The process is free. Update within 60 days of an address change. Your EIN number does not change — only the address on file. Processing takes 4-6 weeks.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>How to Change</Label>
## How to Update <Accent>EIN Address</Accent>

### Method 1: Form 8822-B (Recommended)
1. Download Form 8822-B from irs.gov
2. Complete Part I (EIN, business name, old address)
3. Complete Part II (new address)
4. Sign and date
5. Mail to address in form instructions

### Method 2: Letter to IRS
Write a letter including: EIN, entity name, old address, new address, responsible party signature. Mail to Cincinnati, OH 45999.

### Method 3: Next Tax Return
Enter new address on your next federal return. IRS updates automatically.
