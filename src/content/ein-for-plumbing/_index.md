## When Does a Plumbing Business <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

A plumbing business needs an EIN if it is an LLC, corporation, or partnership, or if it hires employees. A solo plumber with no employees and no entity can operate on an SSN, but an EIN is the safer choice for privacy and banking.

The table below maps common plumbing setups to the EIN requirement. Most growing plumbing companies cross into "required" the moment they form an LLC or hire their first apprentice.

| Plumbing Situation | EIN Needed? |
|--------------------|-------------|
| Plumbing LLC or S-corporation | Yes (required) |
| Plumbing partnership (two or more owners) | Yes (required) |
| Solo plumber, sole proprietor, no employees | Recommended |
| Plumbing business with any W-2 employees | Yes (required) |
| Plumbing business with a 401(k) or payroll plan | Yes (required) |
| Non-US owner with a US plumbing LLC | Yes (required) |
| Side jobs as a hobby, no business intent | No |

### Why Plumbing Owners Get an EIN

1. **Payroll** — An EIN is required to hire apprentices and run [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/).
2. **Business banking** — Banks require an EIN to open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) under the company name.
3. **SSN privacy** — An EIN keeps your SSN off supplier credit forms and customer W-9s.
4. **Tax compliance** — The EIN goes on your business tax returns and 1099 reporting.
5. **Credibility** — General contractors and property managers expect a registered business with an EIN.

## How Does a Non-US Resident File <Accent>Form SS-4 for a Plumbing LLC?</Accent>

A non-US resident files Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, entering a passport number on Line 7b instead of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US Plumbing LLC">Register an LLC for your plumbing supply, fixture import, or services company through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from outside the US.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your valid passport, your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your foreign address. No SSN or ITIN is required to get the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your LLC name on Line 1, your foreign address on Line 4, your full legal name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. Enter "plumbing" or "plumbing services" as the principal activity on Line 16.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### Plumbing SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Plumbing Business Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US plumbing LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your business or foreign mailing address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number (non-residents) |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | "Plumbing" or "Plumbing services" |
