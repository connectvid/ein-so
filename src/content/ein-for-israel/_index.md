## How Do Israeli Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Israeli citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using an Israeli passport number in place of an SSN on Line 7b. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only path for Israeli applicants. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Israeli founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Israel.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Israeli passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Israeli address. No Company Number or Israeli registration is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Israeli address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Israeli passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by email through ein.so.</Step>
</Steps>
