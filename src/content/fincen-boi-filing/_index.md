<Capsule>**FinCEN's BOI e-filing system at fincen.gov/boi is the only authorized platform for filing Beneficial Ownership Information reports.** Filing is free. The system is operated by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN), a bureau of the US Department of the Treasury. Required by the Corporate Transparency Act (CTA).</Capsule>
</Section>

### How to Access
1. Go to fincen.gov/boi
2. No account needed for filing
3. Select 'File BOIR' (Beneficial Ownership Information Report)
4. Complete the form online
5. Submit and save confirmation

## Federal <Accent>Requirements</Accent>

The Corporate Transparency Act requires reporting companies to disclose:
- Company information (name, address, EIN, state of formation)
- All beneficial owners (25%+ ownership or substantial control)
- Company applicant (for entities formed after Jan 1, 2024)

See [BOI requirements](/boi-filing-requirements/) for who must file and exemptions.
