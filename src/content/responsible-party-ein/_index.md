<Section>
<Capsule>**The responsible party on Form SS-4 must be an individual (not a business entity) who controls, manages, or directs the entity.** For LLCs: the managing member. For corporations: a principal officer (president, CEO). For trusts: the trustee. For estates: the executor. The IRS requires the responsible party's SSN, ITIN, or passport number on Line 7b.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>Who Is It</Label>
## Who Is the <Accent>Responsible Party?</Accent>

| Entity Type | Responsible Party |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Single-member LLC | The sole member |
| Multi-member LLC | Managing member |
| Corporation | Principal officer (president, CEO) |
| Partnership | General partner |
| Trust | Trustee |
| Estate | Executor/administrator |
| Nonprofit | President or principal officer |

**Key rule:** Must be an individual. Cannot be another LLC, corporation, or entity.
</Section>

<Section>
<Label>Rules</Label>
## Responsible Party <Accent>Rules</Accent>

1. **Must be an individual** — not a business
2. **Must control or manage** the entity
3. **Must provide SSN, ITIN, or passport number** on Line 7b
4. **Can be changed** by filing Form 8822-B
5. **Must be updated** within 60 days of a change

For non-residents: use your passport number on Line 7b. No SSN needed.
</Section>
