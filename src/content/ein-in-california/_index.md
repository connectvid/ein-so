### Federal EIN vs California State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and California state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **California State Tax ID** — From Franchise Tax Board, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
