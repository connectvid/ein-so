<Capsule>**IRS Letter 147C is an EIN verification letter that confirms your EIN, entity name, and address on file.** Request it by calling the IRS at 800-829-4933. The IRS faxes Letter 147C within 24-48 hours or mails it within 10 business days. Banks and financial institutions accept Letter 147C as proof of your EIN.</Capsule>

<Label>How to Request</Label>
## How to Request <Accent>Letter 147C</Accent>

1. Call IRS at **800-829-4933** (or 267-941-1099 international)
2. Navigate to EIN verification
3. Verify identity as responsible party
4. Request **fax** (faster) or **mail**
5. Provide fax number or confirm mailing address
6. Receive within **24-48 hours** (fax) or **10 days** (mail)

**Tip:** Always request by fax for fastest delivery.
