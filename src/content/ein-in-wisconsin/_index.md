### Federal EIN vs Wisconsin State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Wisconsin state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Wisconsin State Tax ID** — From Department of Revenue, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
