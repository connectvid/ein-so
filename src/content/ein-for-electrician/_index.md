<Capsule>**Electricians need an EIN if they operate as an LLC, corporation, or hire employees.** Electrical contractors need an EIN for licensing, bonding, and employee payroll requirements. The EIN is free from the IRS. Sole proprietors can use their SSN but an EIN is recommended for separating business and personal finances. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

### Why Electricians Get an EIN

1. **Tax compliance** — Required on business tax returns
2. **Business banking** — Open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
3. **Privacy** — Protect your SSN on invoices and W-9s
4. **Credibility** — Professional business identity
5. **Hiring** — Required for [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/)
