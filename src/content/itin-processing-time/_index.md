<Capsule>**ITIN processing takes 7-11 weeks from when the IRS receives your Form W-7.** Peak season (January-April): 9-11 weeks. Off-season: 7 weeks. The IRS does not offer expedited ITIN processing. Using a Certifying Acceptance Agent (CAA) does not speed up processing but lets you keep your original passport.</Capsule>
</Section>

## Can You Speed It Up?

The IRS does not offer expedited ITIN processing. However:
- Apply **off-season** (May-December) for faster processing
- Use a **CAA** to avoid document return delays
- Ensure **complete, error-free** application to prevent resubmission
