## How Do Tunisian Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Tunisian citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a Tunisia passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Tunisian founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Tunisia.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Tunisia passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Tunisia address. No Matricule Fiscal or Tunisian registration is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Tunisia address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Tunisia passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent errors. See the [SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/).</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at [855-215-1627](/irs-fax-number-for-ein/). Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### Tunisia-Specific SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Tunisian Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your Tunisia address (street, postal code, governorate) |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your Tunisia passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "e-commerce," "consulting," "software" |
