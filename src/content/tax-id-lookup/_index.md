<Section>
<Capsule>**Look up a Tax ID number using IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search (nonprofits), SEC EDGAR (public companies), state Secretary of State databases, or by requesting a W-9 form.** There is no universal public tax ID lookup tool. The IRS protects tax identification numbers as confidential information.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>Methods</Label>
## Tax ID Lookup <Accent>Methods</Accent>

| Method | Finds | Cost |
|--------|-------|------|
| IRS Tax Exempt Search | Nonprofit EINs | Free |
| SEC EDGAR | Public company EINs | Free |
| State SOS databases | Registered business EINs | Free |
| W-9 request | Any business EIN | Free |

For EIN-specific lookup, see [EIN lookup](/ein-lookup/) and [EIN lookup by name](/ein-lookup-by-name/).
</Section>

<Section>
<Label>Limitations</Label>
## Why Tax ID Lookup <Accent>Is Limited</Accent>

Tax IDs are protected under IRC Section 6103. The IRS does not offer a public search for:
- Private company EINs
- Individual SSNs
- Individual ITINs

To find a private company's tax ID, request IRS Form W-9 directly from the company.
