### Federal EIN vs Maryland State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Maryland state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Maryland State Tax ID** — From Comptroller of Maryland, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
