## Is a Maryland EIN Different From a <Accent>Federal EIN?</Accent>

A Maryland EIN is not a separate thing. The EIN is a federal number issued by the IRS, identical in all 50 states. Maryland does not issue its own EIN. The same 9-digit number (XX-XXXXXXX) works nationwide for federal tax reporting.

Maryland does run its own state-level tax registration, which is separate from the federal EIN. A Maryland business can need both numbers depending on its activities. The table below separates the two.

| Identifier | Issued by | Used for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal taxes, banking, payment processors, Form 5472 |
| Maryland state tax registration | Comptroller of Maryland | State sales tax, withholding, employer accounts |

Your federal EIN does not replace Maryland state tax registration, and the state registration does not replace the EIN. Most non-resident-owned LLCs with no US employees and no Maryland sales tax nexus need only the federal EIN to bank and file. Confirm your Maryland registration duties with a CPA. See [state tax ID](/state-tax-id/) for more on the distinction.

## How Do You Get an EIN <Accent>in Maryland?</Accent>

You get an EIN in Maryland by filing IRS Form SS-4. The IRS charges $0. Four methods exist, but non-residents without an SSN must use fax to 855-215-1627, because the IRS online tool requires an SSN.

| Method | Speed | Cost | SSN required |
|--------|-------|------|--------------|
| [Online](/ein-online-application/) | Instant | $0 | Yes |
| [Fax](/ein-by-fax/) | 4-7 days | $0 (DIY) / $49 (ein.so) | No |
| [Phone](/ein-by-phone/) | Same day | $0 | International call |
| [Mail](/ein-by-mail/) | 4-6 weeks | $0 | No |

The fax method is the standard route for non-residents. ein.so prepares Form SS-4, faxes it to the IRS at 855-215-1627, and returns your EIN assignment letter by email. Standard processing costs $49 (4-7 business days). Express costs $97 (2-3 business days). See [how to get an EIN](/how-to-get-ein/) for the complete walkthrough and [EIN without SSN](/ein-without-ssn/) for the non-resident path.

### What Non-Residents Do After Forming a Maryland LLC

1. Form the LLC with the Maryland State Department of Assessments and Taxation, usually through a registered agent

## How Do Non-Residents File Form SS-4 for a <Accent>Maryland LLC?</Accent>

Non-residents file Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627 and enter a passport number on Line 7b in place of an SSN. The form takes minutes to complete correctly. The IRS issues most error-free EINs within 4-7 business days and returns the CP 575 assignment letter.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your Maryland LLC">Register your LLC with the Maryland State Department of Assessments and Taxation, usually through a registered agent with a Maryland address. This creates the legal entity that the EIN attaches to. You can do this online from outside the US. Confirm current state filing fees on the official site.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your valid passport, your LLC name and Maryland formation details, and your home-country address. No SSN, ITIN, or US address is required to get the EIN itself.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your Maryland LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full legal name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes every line to prevent the errors that cause IRS rejection.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### SS-4 Field Guide for Non-Residents

| SS-4 Field | Non-Resident Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your Maryland LLC name |
| Line 4 (Mailing address) | Your home-country address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason for applying) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "e-commerce," "consulting," "software" |
