## Why Does a Wells Fargo Business Account Need an <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

A Wells Fargo business account needs an EIN because federal banking rules require a business tax ID to open a commercial account for an LLC or corporation. The EIN separates your business identity from your personal identity and lets Wells Fargo report to the IRS.

Banks must verify the legal identity of every business customer under US Know Your Customer rules. For a registered company, that identity is the EIN, not a personal Social Security Number. Wells Fargo uses the EIN to match your account to your IRS records and to file the bank's own information returns. Without an EIN, the branch cannot open a business account in your company's name.

## Can Non-Residents Get an EIN for <Accent>Wells Fargo?</Accent>

Yes. Non-residents get an EIN by filing IRS Form SS-4 with a passport number on Line 7b. No SSN or ITIN is required. The IRS online EIN tool requires an SSN, so non-residents file by fax to 855-215-1627. The IRS issues most error-free EINs in 4-7 business days.

Getting the EIN and opening the Wells Fargo account are two separate steps. The EIN application is fully remote and works from any country. The Wells Fargo branch visit, however, usually requires you to be physically present in the US. Non-residents who cannot travel use online banks instead, which still need the same EIN.

| Step | Where It Happens | Who Can Do It |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Get the EIN | Remote, by fax to the IRS | Any non-resident with a passport |
| Open at a Wells Fargo branch | In-person, inside the US | Applicants who can travel to the US |
| Open at Mercury, Relay, or Wise | Remote, online | Non-residents who stay abroad |

## How Do You Get an EIN for a <Accent>Wells Fargo Account?</Accent>

You get an EIN for a Wells Fargo account in four steps: form your US entity, complete Form SS-4, file it with the IRS, and receive your EIN letter. Non-residents file by fax to 855-215-1627 using a passport number. The IRS issues most EINs in 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC or Corporation">Register your entity in a state such as Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This creates the legal business that the EIN and the Wells Fargo account attach to. You can form the entity online from outside the US.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your business name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. Non-residents skip the SSN field. ein.so completes the form to prevent rejection.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="File Form SS-4 With the IRS">File by fax to 855-215-1627. The IRS charges $0 for the EIN. Standard processing through ein.so delivers in 4-7 business days. Express delivers in 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Open Your Wells Fargo Account">Take your EIN letter, formation documents, Operating Agreement, and passport to a Wells Fargo branch. The account can open the same day once documents are verified. Online banks open in 1-3 business days with the same documents.</Step>
</Steps>
