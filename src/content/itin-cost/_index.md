<Capsule>**The IRS issues ITINs for free. There is no government fee.** Certifying Acceptance Agent (CAA) fees range from $50 to $275. Tax preparers who assist with ITIN applications charge $150-$500. ein.so offers ITIN application service for $197 (Standard) or $297 (Express).</Capsule>

<Section surface>
<Label>IRS Cost</Label>
## IRS ITIN Cost: <Accent>$0 (Free)</Accent>

The IRS charges nothing for ITIN applications. Form W-7 processing is free. The only costs are from third-party assistance:

| Provider | Cost |
|----------|------|
| IRS (direct) | $0 |
| CAA | $50-275 |
| Tax preparer | $150-500 |
| ein.so | $197 standard / $297 express |

Why pay? CAAs let you keep your original passport. Tax preparers handle the 1040-NR. ein.so handles the full process.
</Section>
