## How Do Colombian Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Colombian citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a Colombia passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path for Colombian applicants. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Colombian founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Colombia.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Colombia passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Colombia address. No NIT or Colombian registration is required for the EIN application.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Colombia address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Colombia passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent errors.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter, the CP 575.</Step>
</Steps>

### Colombia-Specific SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Colombian Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your Colombia address (calle, ciudad, código postal) |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your Colombia passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "software," "e-commerce," "consulting" |

## What Mistakes Do Colombian Applicants Make on <Accent>Form SS-4?</Accent>

The top mistakes are using the IRS online tool, leaving Line 7b blank, and formatting the Colombia address in a way the IRS rejects. Each error costs days or weeks of delay and returns no EIN.

<Alert type="warning" title="Avoid these SS-4 errors">
<ol>
<li>Using the IRS online tool, which requires an SSN and blocks non-residents</li>
<li>Leaving Line 7b blank instead of entering your Colombia passport number</li>
