## What Can You Do With Your EIN After <Accent>ein.so Files It?</Accent>

Your EIN lets you open a US business bank account, connect US payment processors, and file US business taxes for your LLC. The EIN is the federal tax ID that links your foreign-owned company to the US financial system. It is identical whether ein.so or Northwest files it.

<NumberedCards>
<Card title="Open a US bank account" index={1}>Mercury, Relay, and Wise accept non-resident LLC owners with an EIN and a passport. You get US account and routing numbers. See [EIN for a bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/).</Card>
<Card title="Accept US payments" index={2}>A US EIN lets you set up Stripe, PayPal, and similar processors on a US entity to bill American customers in dollars.</Card>
<Card title="File Form 5472 each year" index={3}>A foreign-owned single-member US LLC files [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) with a pro-forma Form 1120 annually. The penalty for non-filing is $25,000.</Card>

ein.so files your EIN and gives guidance, not tax advice. Confirm Form 5472, Form 1120, and BOI obligations with a US CPA familiar with non-resident matters.
