### Federal EIN vs Texas State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Texas state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Texas State Tax ID** — From Comptroller of Public Accounts, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
