<Capsule>**Etsy sellers earning $600+ per year receive a 1099-K and need a tax identification number. An EIN separates your Etsy business from personal finances.** If you formed an LLC for your Etsy shop, an EIN is required. Sole proprietors can use their SSN but an EIN is recommended for privacy. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

<Section surface>
<Label>Why Etsy Needs EIN</Label>
## Why Etsy Sellers <Accent>Need an EIN</Accent>

1. **1099-K Reporting** — Etsy issues 1099-K for $600+ in annual sales (since 2022)
2. **Tax Filing** — Report Etsy income on Schedule C or Form 1120
3. **Business Bank Account** — Separate Etsy revenue from personal
4. **Sales Tax Collection** — Some states require EIN for sales tax registration
5. **Wholesale Buying** — Suppliers may require EIN for wholesale orders

### Do You Need an EIN?

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Etsy LLC | Required |
| Etsy sole prop | Recommended |
| Sales $600+/year | Recommended |
| Non-US seller with US LLC | Required |

## How to Get an EIN <Accent>for Etsy</Accent>

1. **US residents:** Apply free online at irs.gov (instant)
2. **Non-residents:** [Fax Form SS-4](/ein-by-fax/) or use [ein.so](/apply/) ($49)
3. **Use EIN for:** Etsy tax settings, bank account, tax returns
