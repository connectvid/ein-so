## How Do Tanzanian Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Tanzanian citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a Tanzania passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Tanzanian founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business entity and a US business address to attach the EIN to. You complete the LLC formation entirely online from Tanzania.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Tanzania passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, and formation date), and your Tanzania address. No TIN or BRELA registration is required for the EIN application.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Tanzania address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Tanzania passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes the [SS-4 form](/ss4-form-guide/) for you to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by mail or fax.</Step>
</Steps>
