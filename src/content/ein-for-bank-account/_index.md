faqs:
  - q: "Can I open a US business bank account with just an EIN?"
    a: "Yes, multiple US banks allow non-residents to open business bank accounts with an EIN and no SSN. Mercury, Relay, and Wise Business are the most popular options for international founders. You will also need your LLC formation documents (Articles of Organization), EIN confirmation letter (CP 575), operating agreement, and a valid passport or government-issued ID."
  - q: "Which US bank is best for non-residents with an EIN?"
    a: "Mercury is the top choice for most non-resident founders. It offers free business checking, no minimum balance, seamless online onboarding, and integrates with Stripe, QuickBooks, and other business tools. Relay is an excellent alternative with fee-free banking and intuitive budgeting features. Wise Business works best if you need multi-currency accounts and low-cost international transfers."
  - q: "Do I need to visit the US to open a business bank account?"
    a: "No. Mercury, Relay, and Wise Business all allow you to open an account 100% online from anywhere in the world. You complete the application online, upload your documents digitally, and receive your account details by email. No in-person visit to a US bank branch is required."
  - q: "How long does it take to open a US business bank account with an EIN?"
    a: "Application approval takes 1 to 5 business days depending on the bank. Mercury usually approves accounts within 1-2 business days. Relay and Wise may take 2-5 business days. After approval, you can start receiving and sending payments immediately through online banking."
  - q: "Can I open a personal US bank account with an EIN?"
    a: "No. An EIN is a business tax identification number and cannot be used to open a personal bank account. Personal accounts require an SSN or ITIN. If you need a personal US bank account as a non-resident, you will need to apply for an ITIN or visit a bank branch in person with your passport."
  - q: "What documents do banks require to open a business account with an EIN?"
    a: "Most banks require: (1) EIN confirmation letter (IRS Form CP 575 or 147C), (2) Articles of Organization or Certificate of Formation, (3) Operating Agreement for your LLC, (4) valid passport or government-issued photo ID of all owners, and (5) proof of business address. Some banks also require a brief description of your business activities."
  - q: "Can I get a debit card with my US business bank account?"
    a: "Yes. Mercury, Relay, and most US business banks issue physical and virtual debit cards linked to your business checking account. Mercury provides unlimited free virtual cards instantly and ships a physical card to your international address. Virtual cards are available immediately upon account approval."
  - q: "Do I need an LLC to open a US business bank account?"
    a: "While sole proprietors can open business accounts at some banks, most online banks that serve non-residents require a US-registered LLC or corporation. Forming a US LLC (most choose Wyoming or Delaware) gives you the legal entity needed to open a business bank account, get an EIN, and operate professionally in the US market."
