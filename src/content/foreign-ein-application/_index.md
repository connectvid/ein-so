<Capsule>**Submit a foreign EIN application by completing IRS Form SS-4 and faxing it to 855-215-1627.** International applicants use their passport number instead of an SSN on Line 7b. The IRS processes foreign applications within 4-7 business days. ein.so prepares and files your SS-4 for $49.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Label>Line by Line</Label>
## Form SS-4 for <Accent>Foreign Applicants</Accent>

### Critical Fields

| Line | Field | Foreign Applicant Entry |
|------|-------|-------------------------|
| 1 | Legal name | Your LLC/entity name |
| 2 | Trade name | DBA if different |
| 3 | Executor/trustee | Your personal name |
| 4a-4b | Mailing address | Your foreign address |
| 7a | Responsible party | Your name (individual) |
| 7b | SSN/ITIN | **Passport number** |
| 9a | Type of entity | LLC/Corporation/etc |
| 10 | Reason | Started new business |
| 16 | Country | Your country of residence |
| 18 | Third party designee | ein.so (if using service) |

For complete instructions, see [SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/). For [responsible party](/responsible-party-ein/) guidance.

## Common <Accent>Mistakes</Accent>

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Using foreign tax ID on Line 7b | Use **passport number** |
| Leaving Line 7b blank | Must provide identification |
| Wrong entity type | Match your LLC/corp registration |
| US address on Line 4 | Use your actual foreign address |
| Missing signature | Sign the form |
