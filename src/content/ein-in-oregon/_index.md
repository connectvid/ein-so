## Is an Oregon EIN Different From a <Accent>Federal EIN?</Accent>

No. An Oregon EIN is identical to a federal EIN because the IRS issues one number used in all 50 states. Oregon does not create its own EIN. The same 9-digit number (XX-XXXXXXX) works whether your business operates in Portland, Eugene, or outside the United States.

Confusion arises because Oregon maintains a separate state tax registration through the Oregon Department of Revenue. That state registration is not an EIN. The two serve different purposes.

| Identifier | Issuer | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal tax filings, banking, hiring, Form SS-4 |
| Oregon state tax ID | Oregon Department of Revenue | Oregon payroll, withholding, business taxes |

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration, and your state registration does not replace the EIN. Register with the Oregon Department of Revenue only after the IRS issues your EIN. See [state tax ID](/state-tax-id/) for the distinction. Confirm your exact Oregon registration duties with a CPA.

## How Do You Apply for an EIN in <Accent>Oregon?</Accent>

You apply for an EIN in Oregon through the IRS, not the state. US residents with an SSN use the instant online tool at irs.gov. Non-residents without an SSN submit Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, entering a passport number on Line 7b. The IRS issues most correct filings within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your Oregon LLC">Register your LLC with the Oregon Secretary of State, or form in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico if you prefer those states. This gives you a legal entity and a US business address to attach the EIN to. Non-residents complete this online.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your address. No SSN or ITIN is required. A passport number alone satisfies Line 7b for non-residents.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent the errors that trigger IRS rejection.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit and Receive Your EIN">US residents submit online for an instant EIN. Non-residents fax the SS-4 to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

## What Are the Tax and Compliance Steps for an <Accent>Oregon Business?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member Oregon LLC must file Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. ## What Do You Do After Getting an EIN in <Accent>Oregon?</Accent>

After getting an EIN in Oregon, you open a US bank account, file your BOI report, register with the Oregon Department of Revenue, and set up payment processing. The EIN unlocks each step. Foreign-owned LLCs then file Form 5472 annually to avoid the $25,000 penalty.

<li>Register with the Oregon Department of Revenue for state taxes if your activities require it.</li>
