## How Fast Does Each Service <Accent>Deliver an EIN?</Accent>

ein.so delivers an EIN in 4-7 business days on Standard ($49) and 2-3 business days on Express ($97). Harbor Compliance does not publish a fixed non-resident turnaround. The real constraint for every provider is IRS fax processing time, which runs 4-7 business days for an error-free Form SS-4.

No service can make the IRS go faster than the IRS goes. The fax method takes 4-7 business days when the form has no errors. Speed differences come from how quickly a provider prepares and submits your SS-4, and how it handles IRS follow-up.

The EIN is the starting point, not the end of US compliance. Several recurring obligations follow, and the right help depends on your situation.
