## How Fast Does Each Service Deliver Your <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

ein.so delivers a Standard EIN in 4-7 business days for $49 and an Express EIN in 2-3 business days for $97. LegalZoom's EIN timing depends on the package and order volume, so confirm current speed on legalzoom.com before ordering against a deadline.

The IRS sets the real processing floor. A correctly completed Form SS-4 faxed to 855-215-1627 returns an EIN assignment letter within 4-7 business days. Errors restart the clock. The biggest delay for non-residents is a malformed international address, which the IRS rejects without warning. ein.so reformats foreign addresses to an IRS-accepted style before filing.

## Which Service Is Built for <Accent>Non-US Residents?</Accent>

ein.so is built specifically for non-US residents who do not have an SSN. LegalZoom is a broad US legal-services company whose EIN add-on is designed around US-based founders. If you apply from outside the US without an SSN, the non-resident path is the only path that works.

The IRS online EIN tool requires an SSN. Most non-residents do not have one, so the online tool is blocked. The fallback is Form SS-4 filed by fax to 855-215-1627, with a passport number on Line 7b in place of the SSN. This is the exact workflow ein.so runs every day.

## Does ein.so or LegalZoom Handle Both EIN and <Accent>ITIN?</Accent>

ein.so handles both. ein.so files EINs at $49 Standard or $97 Express, and ITINs at $197 Standard or $297 Express. An EIN is a business tax ID for your LLC. An ITIN is a personal tax ID for an individual. They are different numbers with different uses.

Non-residents often confuse the two. You do not need an ITIN to get an EIN. Your passport number on Form SS-4 Line 7b satisfies the EIN application. You need an ITIN only when you must report personal US tax obligations or claim certain treaty benefits as an individual. Confirm which applies to you with a CPA.

| Identifier | What it is | ein.so price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EIN | Business tax ID for your US LLC | $49 / $97 |
| ITIN | Personal tax ID for an individual | $197 / $297 |
| SSN | US resident personal ID | Not issued to most non-residents |

For most non-resident founders forming a US LLC, the EIN alone is enough to [open a US bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) and run the business. Start with the EIN through [the application page](/apply/).
