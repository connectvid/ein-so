<Section>
<Capsule>**A Tax Identification Number (TIN) is the umbrella term for all US tax identification numbers.** TIN includes: EIN (businesses), SSN (US citizens), ITIN (non-residents without SSN), PTIN (tax preparers), and ATIN (adopted children). When a form asks for your TIN, provide whichever number matches your situation.</Capsule>
</Section>
