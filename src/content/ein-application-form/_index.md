<Capsule>**The EIN application form is IRS Form SS-4 (Application for Employer Identification Number).** Download it free from irs.gov. The current version is Rev. December 2023. You can submit Form SS-4 online (instant, SSN required), by fax (4-7 days), by phone (same day), or by mail (4-6 weeks).</Capsule>
