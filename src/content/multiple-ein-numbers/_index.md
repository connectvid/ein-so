<Section>
<Capsule>**Each business entity gets one unique EIN. One person can have multiple EINs for multiple businesses.** If you own 3 LLCs, you need 3 EINs. There is no limit. You need a new EIN when you form a new entity or change entity types. You do NOT need a new EIN for name or address changes.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>The Rule</Label>
## One Entity = <Accent>One EIN</Accent>

| Situation | EINs |
|-----------|------|
| 1 LLC | 1 |
| 2 LLCs | 2 |
| 1 LLC + 1 Corp | 2 |
| 5 businesses | 5 |

### When You Need a NEW EIN

| Event | New EIN? |
|-------|----------|
| New LLC/Corp | Yes |
| Sole prop to LLC | Yes |
| New sole prop owner | Yes |
| Bankruptcy | Yes |
