<Label>Federal vs State</Label>

## Is an Iowa EIN Different From a <Accent>Federal EIN?</Accent>

No. An Iowa EIN is the same as a federal EIN because the IRS issues one EIN that works in every state. Iowa does not create its own EIN. Your 9-digit number stays the same whether you operate in Iowa or relocate to another state.

Two different identifiers confuse many Iowa business owners. The federal EIN comes from the IRS for federal tax purposes. The Iowa state tax ID comes from the Iowa Department of Revenue for state sales tax and employer withholding. They serve separate functions and you may need both.

| Identifier | Issued By | Used For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal taxes, banking, Form SS-4 filing |
| Iowa state tax ID | Iowa Department of Revenue | Iowa sales tax, employer withholding |

Your EIN does not replace Iowa state registration. If you sell taxable goods or hire employees in Iowa, register separately with the Department of Revenue. Confirm your exact obligations with the state or a CPA, since registration needs depend on your activity. See [state tax ID](/state-tax-id/) for more detail on the distinction.

## How Do You Get an EIN <Accent>in Iowa?</Accent>

You get an EIN in Iowa by filing IRS Form SS-4 in one of four ways. The online tool is instant but needs an SSN. Fax to 855-215-1627 takes 4-7 business days. Phone suits international applicants. Mail takes 4 or more weeks. The method depends on whether you have an SSN.

| Method | Speed | SSN Required | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Online](/ein-online-application/) | Instant | Yes | $0 |
| [Fax](/ein-by-fax/) | 4-7 days | No | $0 / $49 via ein.so |
| [Phone](/ein-by-phone/) | Same day | No | $0 |
| [Mail](/ein-by-mail/) | 4+ weeks | No | $0 |

The process is identical regardless of state. Iowa adds no extra EIN steps. See [how to get an EIN](/how-to-get-ein/) for complete instructions and the [SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/) for line-by-line help.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your Iowa Entity">Register your LLC or corporation with the Iowa Secretary of State, or use an existing entity. Non-residents can form an Iowa LLC online through a registered agent. The EIN attaches to this legal entity.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your entity name, formation date, and a responsible party. US residents provide an SSN. Non-residents provide a valid, unexpired passport. No Iowa registration number is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your entity name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, the responsible party on Line 7a, and your SSN or passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit and Receive Your EIN">File online for an instant EIN, or fax to 855-215-1627 and wait 4-7 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter, the CP 575.</Step>
</Steps>

## Can a Non-US Resident Get an EIN for an <Accent>Iowa LLC?</Accent>

Yes. A non-US resident gets an EIN for an Iowa LLC without an SSN or ITIN. You enter your passport number on Form SS-4 Line 7b instead of an SSN. The IRS online tool is blocked without an SSN, so non-residents fax Form SS-4 to 855-215-1627.

Owning an Iowa LLC as a non-resident is fully legal. The IRS does not require US presence, residency, or citizenship to assign an EIN. Many international founders pick Iowa or another state, form an LLC remotely, and apply for the EIN by fax from abroad.

| SS-4 Field | Non-Resident Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your Iowa LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your foreign or US mailing address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number (no SSN needed) |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |

A common rejection cause is malformed international address formatting on Line 4. The IRS silently rejects addresses it cannot parse. ein.so reformats your address to an IRS-accepted style. See [EIN without SSN](/ein-without-ssn/) and [EIN for non-residents](/ein-for-non-residents/) for full guidance. If you also need a personal taxpayer number, ein.so files ITIN applications for $197 (Standard) or $297 (Express).

## What Should Iowa Business Owners Know About <Accent>EIN Taxes?</Accent>

Your EIN handles federal tax reporting, while Iowa state taxes run through a separate state registration. The EIN does not create a tax bill by itself. A foreign-owned single-member Iowa LLC is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax purposes.

<Alert type="warning" title="Iowa LLC federal filing duties">
<ol>
<li>A foreign-owned single-member Iowa LLC files Form 5472 plus a pro-forma Form 1120 every year.</li>
<li>The penalty for failing to file Form 5472 is $25,000.</li>
<li>Form 5472 is an information return, not always a tax payment.</li>
<li>ein.so files the EIN application only and does not give tax advice.</li>
</ol>
</Alert>
