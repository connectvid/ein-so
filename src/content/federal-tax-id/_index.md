<Capsule>**A Federal Tax ID and an EIN are the same 9-digit number from the IRS.** When people say Federal Tax ID for a business, they mean the Employer Identification Number (EIN). Format: XX-XXXXXXX. Apply free at irs.gov (SSN required) or through ein.so for $49 (non-residents).</Capsule>
