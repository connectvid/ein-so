## How Does a West Virginia EIN Differ From a <Accent>State Tax ID?</Accent>

A West Virginia EIN does not differ from a federal EIN — they are the same number. The EIN comes from the IRS and works nationwide. A West Virginia state tax ID is a separate registration from the West Virginia State Tax Department for state-level taxes.

Many business owners confuse the two. The federal EIN identifies your business to the IRS for federal tax filings, banking, and payroll. The West Virginia state tax registration identifies your business to the state for any applicable state taxes. Your EIN does not replace state registration, and your state registration does not replace the EIN.

| ID Type | Issued By | Used For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal taxes, Form SS-4, banking, Form 5472 |
| WV State Tax ID | WV State Tax Department | State-level tax obligations |
| LLC registration | WV Secretary of State | Legal entity formation, annual reports |

A non-resident-owned West Virginia LLC always needs the federal EIN. Whether you also need the state tax registration depends on your activities in West Virginia. Confirm your exact obligations with a CPA. See our [state tax ID](/state-tax-id/) page for more detail.

## How Do Non-Residents Get an EIN for a <Accent>West Virginia LLC?</Accent>

Non-residents get an EIN for a West Virginia LLC by faxing IRS Form SS-4 to 855-215-1627 with a passport number on Line 7b. No SSN or ITIN is needed. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only path for most foreign founders.

The federal SS-4 process is stable and identical in every state. Your West Virginia address or registered agent address goes on the form, but the IRS treats the application the same way it treats one from any state.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your West Virginia LLC">Register the LLC through the West Virginia Secretary of State, using a registered agent with a West Virginia address. This creates the legal entity that the EIN attaches to. You can complete formation online from outside the US.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC name, formation state (West Virginia), and formation date. No SSN, ITIN, or US address of your own is required. The registered agent address serves as the business address.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your LLC name on Line 1, the business address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes the SS-4 to prevent rejection. See our [SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/).</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

## What Tax Filings Does a Foreign-Owned <Accent>West Virginia LLC Face?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member West Virginia LLC files Form 5472 plus a pro-forma Form 1120 with the IRS each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. The LLC also files West Virginia annual reports and may owe state taxes depending on its activity.

The EIN is the starting point for all of this compliance. Without it, you cannot file the federal information returns or register for state taxes.
