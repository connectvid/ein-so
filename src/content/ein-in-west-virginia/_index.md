### Federal EIN vs West Virginia State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and West Virginia state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **West Virginia State Tax ID** — From State Tax Department, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
