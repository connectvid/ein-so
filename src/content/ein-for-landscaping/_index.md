## Do Landscaping Businesses <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

Landscaping businesses need an EIN when they form an LLC or corporation, hire employees, or open a business bank account. Sole proprietors with no employees can use an SSN, but an EIN protects that number and adds credibility on commercial work.

The table below maps common landscaping situations to the EIN requirement.

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Landscaping LLC or corporation | Yes (required) |
| Sole proprietor with W-2 employees | Yes (required) |
| Sole proprietor, no employees | Recommended |
| Non-US resident with a US landscaping LLC | Yes (required) |
| Opening a business bank account | Yes (banks require it) |
| Bidding on commercial or municipal contracts | Yes (W-9 requires it) |
| Hobby mowing for cash only | No |

### Why Landscaping Businesses Get an EIN

1. **Commercial contracts** — Property managers and HOAs require an EIN on the W-9 before they pay you.
2. **Payroll** — Hiring crew requires an EIN for [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/) and federal withholding.
3. **Equipment financing** — Lenders underwrite mowers, trucks, and trailers against the business.
4. **Business banking** — Banks require an EIN to open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/).
5. **Privacy** — An EIN keeps your SSN off invoices and vendor forms.

## How Does a Landscaping Business <Accent>Get an EIN?</Accent>

A landscaping business gets an EIN by filing IRS Form SS-4. US residents apply online at irs.gov for an instant EIN using an SSN. Non-residents and SSN-free owners fax the SS-4 to 855-215-1627, which takes 4-7 business days.

The IRS charges $0 for an EIN through every channel. The cost difference between methods is speed and convenience, not the EIN itself.

## How Do You File Form SS-4 for a <Accent>Landscaping Business?</Accent>

You file Form SS-4 by entering your landscaping business name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, the responsible party on Line 7a, and an SSN or passport number on Line 7b. Non-residents fax the form to 855-215-1627. The IRS issues most correct EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Choose Your Business Structure">Decide between a sole proprietorship and an LLC. An [LLC](/ein-for-llc/) protects personal assets, which matters when crews operate heavy equipment near client property. A [sole proprietorship](/ein-for-sole-proprietor/) is simpler but offers no liability shield.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">Collect your legal name, business name, business address, and tax ID. US owners use an SSN. Non-resident owners use a valid passport number. Have your LLC formation date ready if you formed an entity.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter the landscaping LLC name on Line 1, the address on Line 4, your full name on Line 7a, and your SSN or passport number on Line 7b. Mark "Started new business" on Line 10. ein.so completes this to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit and Receive Your EIN">US residents submit online for an instant number. Non-residents fax the SS-4 to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

## What Should a Landscaping Owner Know About <Accent>EIN Taxes?</Accent>

A US-based single-member landscaping LLC is a disregarded entity by default, so income flows to the owner's personal return. A foreign-owned single-member US landscaping LLC files Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year, and the penalty for not filing is $25,000.

<NumberedCards>
<Card title="US-Based LLC Filing" index={1}>A US owner reports single-member LLC landscaping income on Schedule C of the personal return. The EIN identifies the business on payroll and information returns. Confirm your filing path with a US CPA.</Card>
<Card title="Foreign-Owned LLC: Form 5472" index={2}>A foreign-owned single-member US LLC must file [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The non-filing penalty is $25,000. This is an information return, not automatically a tax bill.</Card>
<Card title="BOI Report to FinCEN" index={3}>Most US LLCs report beneficial ownership to FinCEN. Review the [BOI filing rules](/boi-filing/) to track current requirements and deadlines for your landscaping LLC.</Card>
<Card title="Need an ITIN Too?" index={4}>Some non-resident owners need an ITIN for personal filing alongside the business EIN. ein.so offers ITIN service at $197 Standard and $297 Express. Confirm whether you need one with a CPA.</Card>
