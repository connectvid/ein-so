## How Do Lebanese Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Lebanese citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a Lebanon passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Lebanese founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Lebanon.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Lebanon passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Lebanon address. No Tax Number or local registration is required for the EIN application.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Lebanon address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Lebanon passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent errors.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by mail and ein.so emails your number.</Step>
</Steps>

### Lebanon-Specific SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Lebanese Entry |
| --- | --- |

## What Should Lebanese Entrepreneurs Know About <Accent>US Taxes?</Accent>

A single-member US LLC owned by a Lebanese resident is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax, meaning the LLC itself pays no US federal income tax on non-US-sourced income. The owner still must file annual information returns with the IRS to stay compliant.

## How Does Lebanon's Tax ID Compare to a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Lebanon's Tax Number from the MOF identifies a business inside Lebanon, while a US EIN identifies a business inside the United States. Neither replaces the other. A Lebanese entrepreneur operating a US LLC needs the US EIN regardless of any Lebanese registration.
