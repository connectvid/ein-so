### Federal EIN vs Rhode Island State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Rhode Island state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Rhode Island State Tax ID** — From Division of Taxation, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
