## The Problem <Accent>We Solve</Accent>

### The IRS Online System Blocks Non-Residents

The IRS online EIN application **requires a Social Security Number (SSN) or ITIN**. If you are a non-US resident without either, you cannot use the online system. That locks out millions of international entrepreneurs.

### Faxing Form SS-4 Is Error-Prone and Slow

Your only option is to **fax Form SS-4** to the IRS international fax line. But the form is confusing, the instructions are unclear, and a single mistake can mean rejection and weeks of delay.

### Hundreds of SS-4 Forms Filed Successfully

We have filed hundreds of SS-4 forms for non-resident applicants across 50+ countries: LLCs, sole proprietors, partnerships, and more. We handle the fax, the follow-up, and the delivery.

**You provide the information. We do everything else.**
