<Capsule>**Check your EIN application status by calling the IRS at 800-829-4933 (domestic) or 267-941-1099 (international).** The IRS does not offer online status checking for EIN applications. Online applicants get instant EINs. Fax applicants should call after 7 business days. Mail applicants should call after 6 weeks.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>How to Check</Label>
## Check EIN Status <Accent>by Phone</Accent>

**Call:** 800-829-4933 (domestic) / 267-941-1099 (international)
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 7am-7pm ET

Have ready: entity name, responsible party name, application date, method used.

**There is no online status checker.** You must call.
