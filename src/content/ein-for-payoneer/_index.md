<Capsule>**Payoneer requires a tax identification number to set up US payment receiving accounts. An EIN (Employer Identification Number) connects your US business entity to your Payoneer account.** International freelancers and businesses use Payoneer with their EIN to receive USD payments from US clients. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

<Label>Why Payoneer Needs EIN</Label>
## Why Payoneer Users <Accent>Need an EIN</Accent>

1. **US Receiving Account** — Payoneer requires a tax ID for US payment accounts
2. **Tax Compliance** — IRS requires tax ID for US-source income reporting
3. **Business Verification** — Payoneer verifies your business entity with your EIN
4. **Higher Limits** — Business accounts with EIN get higher transaction limits

### Who Needs an EIN for Payoneer?

| User Type | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| US LLC receiving US payments | Yes |
| Freelancer with US LLC | Yes |
| Non-US individual (no LLC) | No (use personal tax ID) |
| Business receiving US clients | Recommended |

## How to Connect <Accent>EIN to Payoneer</Accent>

1. Get your EIN — [Apply through ein.so](/apply/) ($49)
