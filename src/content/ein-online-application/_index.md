<Section>
<Capsule>**The IRS EIN Assistant at irs.gov lets US residents with an SSN or ITIN apply for an EIN online and receive it instantly.** The application takes 10-15 minutes and is available Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM Eastern Time. Non-US residents without an SSN cannot use this tool and must apply by [fax](/ein-by-fax/) or [phone](/ein-by-phone/) instead.</Capsule>
</Section>

## IRS EIN Assistant: <Accent>Step-by-Step Guide</Accent>

<ProcessSteps>
<ProcessStep step="1" title="Go to irs.gov">Navigate to the IRS EIN Assistant page. The direct URL is available on the IRS Small Business page. Do not use third-party websites that mimic the IRS interface.</ProcessStep>
<ProcessStep step="2" title="Select Entity Type">Choose your business structure: sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation, S-corporation, LLC, trust, estate, or other. This determines which IRS forms you will file.</ProcessStep>
<ProcessStep step="3" title="Enter Responsible Party">Provide the name and SSN/ITIN of the responsible party. This must be an individual (not a business). For LLCs, this is typically the managing member. See [responsible party on EIN](/responsible-party-ein/).</ProcessStep>
<ProcessStep step="4" title="Enter Business Details">Provide: legal name, trade name (DBA), business address, county, state, reason for applying, date started, closing month of accounting year, number of employees expected, and principal activity.</ProcessStep>
<ProcessStep step="5" title="Review and Submit">Review all information for accuracy. Submit the application. Your EIN is issued immediately.</ProcessStep>
<ProcessStep step="6" title="Save Your EIN">Download and save the EIN confirmation notice (CP 575). Print a copy for your records. You will need this for [opening a bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) and filing taxes.</ProcessStep>
</ProcessSteps>

## Who Cannot Use the <Accent>Online Application</Accent>

The IRS EIN Assistant has strict limitations:

1. **No SSN or ITIN** — Non-residents without a US tax ID cannot use this tool
2. **Weekend/evening hours** — The system is offline outside Mon-Fri 7am-10pm ET
3. **One per session** — You can only apply for one EIN per responsible party per day
4. **US entities only** — The entity must be located in the US or its territories
5. **Individual responsible party** — The responsible party must be an individual with an SSN/ITIN, not another business

### Alternatives for Non-Residents

If you cannot use the online application, you have three options:

| Method | Speed | Cost |
|--------|-------|------|
| [Fax Form SS-4](/ein-by-fax/) | 4-7 business days | Free (DIY) or $49 (ein.so) |
| [Phone 267-941-1099](/ein-by-phone/) | Same day | Free (+ international calling) |
| [Mail Form SS-4](/ein-by-mail/) | 4-6 weeks | Free |
