<Capsule>**Trucking & Transportation need an EIN if they operate as an LLC, corporation, or hire employees.** Trucking companies need an EIN for DOT registration, IFTA filing, and fleet insurance. The EIN is free from the IRS. Sole proprietors can use their SSN but an EIN is recommended for separating business and personal finances. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

## Do Trucking & Transportation <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Trucking & Transportation LLC | Yes (required) |
| Trucking & Transportation sole proprietor | Recommended |
| Trucking & Transportation with employees | Yes (required) |
| Non-US trucking & transportation with US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Hobby/side income only | No |

### Why Trucking & Transportation Get an EIN

1. **Tax compliance** — Required on business tax returns
2. **Business banking** — Open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
3. **Privacy** — Protect your SSN on invoices and W-9s
4. **Credibility** — Professional business identity
5. **Hiring** — Required for [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/)
