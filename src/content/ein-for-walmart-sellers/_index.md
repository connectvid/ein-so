## Do Walmart Marketplace Sellers <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

Walmart Marketplace sellers who operate through a US LLC or corporation need an EIN to complete seller registration and the tax interview. Walmart requires a US tax identification number for the selling entity. An EIN identifies that entity to the IRS.

The answer depends on your business structure. The table below maps the common Walmart seller situations to the EIN requirement.

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Walmart seller operating as a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Walmart seller operating as a corporation | Yes (required) |
| Non-US Walmart seller with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Walmart seller with employees | Yes (required) |
| US sole proprietor selling on Walmart | Recommended |
| Hobby/one-off sales only | No |

### Why Walmart Marketplace Sellers Get an EIN

1. **Seller registration** — Walmart ties your account to a US tax ID
2. **Tax interview (W-9)** — The EIN matches your LLC name to the IRS
3. **Business banking** — Open a [US business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) for payouts
4. **SSN privacy** — Keep your personal SSN off marketplace and W-9 records
5. **Professional identity** — A US entity and EIN signal a credible seller
6. **Hiring** — Required before you run [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/)

A non-US seller almost always needs an EIN because the US LLC, not the individual, registers as the Walmart seller. See [who needs an EIN](/who-needs-ein/) for full eligibility details.

## How Do Non-US Walmart Sellers Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Non-US Walmart sellers submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the path for non-residents. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most non-US Walmart sellers register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You form the LLC online without visiting the US.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (legal name, state, formation date), and your home-country address. No SSN and no ITIN are required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent the errors that cause IRS rejection.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### Walmart Seller SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Walmart Seller Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name (must match the Walmart tax interview) |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your home-country mailing address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as it appears on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | "Retail" or "e-commerce" |

The Line 1 LLC name must match the entity name you enter in the Walmart tax interview exactly. A mismatch between the EIN name and the W-9 name causes TIN verification failures. ein.so checks this alignment before filing. ## What US Taxes Apply to a <Accent>Foreign-Owned Walmart LLC?</Accent>

A single-member US LLC owned by a non-resident is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax, which means the LLC files information returns rather than paying entity-level income tax on non-US-sourced income. Foreign-owned LLCs still carry strict annual filing duties with the IRS.

<NumberedCards>
<Card title="Form 5472 + Pro-Forma 1120" index={1}>A foreign-owned single-member US LLC files [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. It reports transactions between the LLC and its foreign owner. This is an information return, not automatically a tax bill.</Card>
<Card title="US Sales Tax Nexus" index={2}>Walmart collects and remits marketplace sales tax in many US states as the marketplace facilitator. Your own nexus duties depend on inventory location and sales volume. Sales tax rules vary by state and change. Confirm your nexus position with a US CPA.</Card>
<Card title="BOI Report to FinCEN" index={3}>Most US LLCs report beneficial ownership to FinCEN. Review the [BOI filing rules](/boi-filing/) and confirm your deadline. The BOI report is separate from the EIN and from your annual IRS filings.</Card>
<Card title="When You May Need an ITIN" index={4}>An LLC needs only an EIN, not an ITIN. You may need a personal ITIN for individual filing situations or to claim treaty benefits. ein.so files ITINs for $197 Standard and $297 Express. Confirm whether you need one with a US CPA.</Card>
</NumberedCards>

ein.so handles the EIN application and does not provide tax advice. Work with a US CPA familiar with non-resident e-commerce sellers to confirm your Form 5472, sales tax, and BOI obligations. ## What Mistakes Do Walmart Sellers Make <Accent>With the EIN?</Accent>

The most common Walmart seller EIN mistake is a name mismatch between the SS-4 Line 1, the LLC documents, and the Walmart tax interview, which fails TIN verification. The second most common is using the IRS online tool without an SSN, which non-residents cannot complete.

<Alert type="warning" title="Errors that delay your Walmart account">
<ol>
<li>Entity name on the W-9 does not match the EIN name on Line 1 of the SS-4.</li>
<li>Attempting the IRS online tool without an SSN, which the tool requires.</li>
<li>Malformed international address on Line 4, which the IRS silently rejects.</li>
<li>Applying for a second EIN when the existing LLC already has one.</li>
<li>Registering on Walmart before the EIN assignment letter (CP 575) arrives.</li>
</ol>
</Alert>
