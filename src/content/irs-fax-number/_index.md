## IRS Fax Number for <Accent>EIN (Form SS-4)</Accent>

**EIN Application Fax: 855-215-1627**

This number accepts [Form SS-4](/ss4-form-guide/) from all applicants worldwide. The IRS EIN Operation center in Cincinnati, Ohio processes all faxed applications within 4-7 business days.

### Tips for Faxing the IRS

1. Fax during US business hours for fastest processing
2. Include a cover page with your return fax number
3. Keep your transmission confirmation as proof
4. Do NOT fax the same form twice (causes duplicates)
5. Use a reliable fax service or [ein.so](/apply/) ($49)

For non-residents who cannot use the IRS online tool, faxing Form SS-4 is the recommended method. Learn more about [EIN for non-residents](/ein-for-non-residents/) or see [how to get an EIN](/how-to-get-ein/) for all methods.
