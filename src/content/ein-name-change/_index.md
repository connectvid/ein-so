<Capsule>**Change the business name on your EIN by notifying the IRS. LLCs: send a letter to Cincinnati, OH 45999. Corporations: file amended Articles with your state. Sole proprietors: enter the new name on your next tax return.** Your EIN number stays the same. The process is free at the federal level.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>By Entity Type</Label>
## Name Change <Accent>by Entity Type</Accent>

### LLC
1. File amended Articles with your state
2. Send letter to IRS: EIN, old name, new name, state amendment copy, signature
3. Mail to Cincinnati, OH 45999

### Corporation
1. File amended Articles of Incorporation with state
2. Check 'Name change' box on next Form 1120
3. Or send letter to IRS

### Sole Proprietor
1. Enter new name on next Schedule C (Form 1040)
2. No separate IRS notification needed for DBA changes

### Partnership
1. Check 'Name change' box on next Form 1065
