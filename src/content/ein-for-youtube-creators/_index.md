<Label>Why YouTube Needs EIN</Label>
## Why YouTube Creators <Accent>Need an EIN</Accent>

1. **AdSense Tax Compliance** — Google requires tax info (W-9 or W-8BEN) for all monetized channels
2. **Avoid 24% Withholding** — Without a tax ID, Google withholds 24% of US-source income
3. **1099 Reporting** — YouTube issues 1099 for $600+/year in earnings
4. **Business Bank Account** — Separate YouTube income from personal
5. **Business Expenses** — Deduct equipment, software, travel
6. **Professional Credibility** — Brand deals require W-9 with EIN

### Do You Need an EIN?

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|------------|
| Monetized channel (sole prop) | Recommended |
| YouTube LLC | Required |
| Brand sponsorships $600+ | Required (W-9) |
| Non-US creator with US LLC | Required |
| Hobby channel, no income | No |

## Non-US YouTube <Accent>Creators</Accent>

Google withholds up to 30% of US-source AdSense revenue from non-US creators without proper tax documentation. Getting an EIN through a US LLC can:
- Reduce withholding via tax treaty benefits
- Enable US business banking
- Build credibility with US brands
