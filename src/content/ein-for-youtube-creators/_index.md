## Why Do YouTube Creators Need a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

YouTube creators need a US EIN to complete the AdSense tax interview as a business, avoid 24% backup withholding, and open a US business bank account. The EIN is the federal tax ID that links a creator's US LLC to the US financial system.

Google treats AdSense ad revenue as US-source income for the portion earned from US viewers. The platform must collect a tax ID before paying you in full. A creator who runs the channel through a US LLC enters the LLC's EIN, not a personal SSN. This keeps your Social Security Number off 1099 forms and sponsor paperwork.

## Which YouTube Creators <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

A YouTube creator needs an EIN when the channel operates through a US LLC, when a non-US creator forms a US LLC, or when brand deals require a W-9 with a business tax ID. A hobby channel with no income needs no tax ID at all.

The table below maps common creator situations to whether an EIN is required. Sole proprietors can legally use an SSN, but an EIN keeps the SSN private and is free from the IRS.

| Situation | EIN Required? | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hobby channel, no monetization | No | No tax ID needed |
| Monetized channel, US sole proprietor | Optional | SSN works; EIN protects privacy |
| Channel run through a US LLC | Yes | LLC uses its own EIN |
| Non-US creator with a US LLC | Yes | File SS-4 by fax, no SSN |
| Brand sponsorships of $600+ | Yes (W-9) | LLC EIN goes on the W-9 |
| Multiple creators sharing a channel | Yes | Multi-member LLC needs an EIN |

## How Do Non-US YouTube Creators <Accent>Get an EIN?</Accent>

Non-US YouTube creators get an EIN by forming a US LLC and filing IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627. They enter a passport number on Line 7b in place of an SSN. The IRS online EIN tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only path for non-residents.

Google withholds tax only on the US-viewed share of a non-US creator's AdSense revenue. The exact rate depends on whether your country has a US income tax treaty and whether you claim treaty benefits in the AdSense interview. A US LLC and EIN let you complete that interview as a US business entity.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Register an LLC in a state such as Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This creates the US business entity that holds the EIN. You complete this online from any country.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">Collect your valid passport, your LLC name, your LLC formation state and date, and your home address. No SSN, ITIN, or US visit is required.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter the LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent silent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>

## How Does a Creator Fill Out <Accent>Form SS-4?</Accent>

A creator fills out Form SS-4 with the US LLC name, the responsible party's full name, and a passport number on Line 7b for non-residents. Six fields carry the application. ein.so completes every field and reformats international addresses the IRS accepts.

The most common rejection cause for non-US applicants is a malformed international address. The IRS silently rejects addresses that do not fit the SS-4 layout. The table below shows the correct entry for each key field.

| SS-4 Field | Creator Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Mailing address) | Your home or business address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number (non-resident) |
| Line 10 (Reason for applying) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "media," "advertising," "content creation" |

For a US sole-proprietor creator with an SSN, Line 7b takes the SSN and the online tool becomes available. Non-residents must use the fax path. See [EIN without SSN](/ein-without-ssn/) for the passport-based method.

## What Annual Filings Does a <Accent>YouTube LLC</Accent> Have?

A foreign-owned single-member US LLC files Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. Most US LLCs also file a BOI report with FinCEN. These filings attach to the LLC, not to the EIN.
