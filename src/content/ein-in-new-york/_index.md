## Is a New York EIN Different From a Federal <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

A New York EIN is not a separate thing. The EIN is one federal number issued by the IRS, and it is identical in all 50 states. New York does not issue its own EIN. Your 9-digit EIN works the same in New York as in Texas or Florida.

New York does run a separate state tax registration through the Department of Taxation and Finance. That registration is not an EIN. It covers state-level obligations like sales tax, withholding, and payroll. Many New York businesses need both numbers.

| Number | Issued By | Used For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Federal EIN | IRS | Federal tax filing, banking, payroll, vendor forms |
| NY State Tax ID | NY Dept. of Taxation and Finance | State sales tax, state withholding |

Your EIN does not replace state registration, and state registration does not replace your EIN. Start with the EIN, since banks and the IRS require it first. See [how to get an EIN](/how-to-get-ein/) and our [EIN cost breakdown](/ein-cost/) for the full picture.

## How Do You Get an EIN <Accent>in New York?</Accent>

You get an EIN in New York through the IRS using one of four methods. SSN holders use the online tool for an instant number. Applicants without an SSN fax Form SS-4 to 855-215-1627 and receive the EIN in 4-7 business days. The IRS charges $0.

| Method | Speed | SSN Required | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Online](/ein-online-application/) | Instant | Yes | $0 |
| [Fax](/ein-by-fax/) | 4-7 days | No | $0 (DIY) / $49 (ein.so) |
| Phone (800-829-4933) | Same day | Varies | $0 |
| [Mail](/ein-by-mail/) | 4-6 weeks | No | $0 |

The method matters most for non-residents. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so it is blocked for applicants who do not have one. Fax is the practical path. ein.so prepares and faxes the SS-4, then emails your EIN assignment letter.

## Can a Non-Resident Get an EIN for a New York <Accent>LLC?</Accent>

Yes. A non-US resident gets an EIN for a New York LLC without an SSN or ITIN. You enter your passport number on Form SS-4 Line 7b. You file the SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, since the IRS online tool requires an SSN. The IRS issues most error-free filings in 4-7 business days.

The EIN is federal, so your location does not matter. You can form the New York LLC with the Department of State and apply for the EIN from anywhere in the world. Residency, visa status, and citizenship do not change the SS-4 process.

### Non-Resident SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Non-Resident Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your New York LLC name |
| Line 4 (Mailing address) | Your foreign or US mailing address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "software," "e-commerce," "consulting" |

The most common rejection cause for non-residents is address formatting that does not map to the SS-4 layout. ein.so reformats foreign addresses to an IRS-accepted style.
