## How Do Indonesian Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Indonesian citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using an Indonesia passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path for Indonesian applicants. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Indonesian founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Indonesia.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Indonesia passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Indonesia address. No NPWP or Indonesian registration is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Indonesia address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Indonesia passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent errors.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

## How Does an EIN Differ From an <Accent>NPWP in Indonesia?</Accent>

A US EIN and an Indonesian NPWP are separate, unrelated tax numbers. The EIN identifies a US business entity to the IRS. The NPWP identifies an Indonesian taxpayer to the DJP. One does not replace the other.

| Feature | US EIN | Indonesia NPWP |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Issued by | IRS | DJP |
| Format | XX-XXXXXXX (9 digits) | 15 digits |
| Identifies | US business entity | Indonesian taxpayer |
| Cost | $0 from the IRS | Government issued |
| Use in the US | Banking, payments, taxes | None |
| Use in Indonesia | None | Domestic tax filing |

An Indonesian entrepreneur who owns a US LLC holds both numbers. The NPWP covers Indonesian tax obligations. The EIN covers the US LLC's banking, payment processing, and US filings. You apply for the EIN separately through [Form SS-4](/ss4-form-guide/), regardless of your NPWP status.
