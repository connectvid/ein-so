<Capsule>**BOI filing in Florida follows federal FinCEN requirements — it is NOT a state filing.** Florida LLCs and corporations must file their Beneficial Ownership Information report at fincen.gov/boi, not with the Florida Secretary of State. The filing is free. Penalties for non-filing: $500/day.</Capsule>

## BOI Filing in <Accent>Florida</Accent>

### Key Facts
| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| Filed with | FinCEN (federal), NOT Florida SOS |
| URL | fincen.gov/boi |
| Cost | Free |
| Penalty | $500/day for non-filing |
| Who files | All Florida LLCs and corps (unless exempt) |

### Common Confusion
Many Florida business owners confuse BOI filing with state annual reports. They are separate:
- **BOI report** — Filed with FinCEN (federal)
- **Annual report** — Filed with Florida Secretary of State
- Both are required. Neither replaces the other.

## How to File BOI <Accent>in Florida</Accent>
