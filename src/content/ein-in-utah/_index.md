### Federal EIN vs Utah State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and Utah state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **Utah State Tax ID** — From State Tax Commission, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
