## Does Stripe Atlas Give You <Accent>an EIN?</Accent>

Yes. Stripe Atlas files an EIN application as part of its $500 incorporation service, so you do not submit a separate application. Atlas incorporates your Delaware C-Corp or LLC first, then requests the EIN from the IRS on your behalf.

The catch is timing. An EIN is only issued as fast as the IRS processes it, and the IRS treats non-residents differently from US founders.

| Founder type | EIN method | IRS timeline |
| --- | --- | --- |
| US founder with SSN | Online or fax | Instant online; days by fax |
| Non-US founder, no SSN | Form SS-4 by fax | 4-7 business days |
| Non-US founder, mail filing | Form SS-4 by mail | 4-6 weeks |

Stripe Atlas handles the paperwork, but a non-US founder still waits on the IRS. The EIN does not arrive the moment your company is incorporated.

</Section>

## Why Do Stripe Atlas Founders <Accent>Need an EIN Right Away?</Accent>

A Stripe Atlas founder needs the EIN immediately because 4 critical steps depend on it. The company exists on paper after incorporation, but it cannot transact until the EIN arrives.

- **Open the bank account.** Stripe Atlas partners with Mercury, and Mercury requires the EIN to open your business checking account.
- **Activate Stripe payments.** Stripe needs the company EIN to enable live charges and payouts.
- **File the 83(b) election.** Founders electing 83(b) on their shares file within 30 days of share issuance, and the company needs its tax identity in place.
- **File federal taxes.** A Delaware C-Corp files Form 1120, and a foreign-owned LLC files Form 5472. Both require the EIN.

Until the EIN is assigned, the bank account, payment processing, and tax filings all stall. This is why the EIN timeline matters more than founders expect.

</Section>

## How Long Does the Stripe Atlas <Accent>EIN Take?</Accent>

The Stripe Atlas EIN takes several weeks for a non-US founder, because the IRS processes the underlying Form SS-4 by fax or mail. US founders with an SSN receive the EIN within days.

The IRS controls this timeline, not Stripe Atlas. A faxed SS-4 returns an EIN in 4-7 business days when the form is error-free. A mailed SS-4 takes 4-6 weeks. Errors on the form, such as the wrong entity type on Line 8a or a missing signature on Line 18, restart the clock. Filing the SS-4 directly by fax is the fastest non-resident path, which is why founders who need the EIN quickly file it separately.
