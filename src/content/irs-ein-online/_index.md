<Capsule>**The IRS EIN Assistant at irs.gov issues EINs instantly online.** Available Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM Eastern Time. Requires a valid SSN or ITIN. The process takes 10-15 minutes. Non-residents without SSN cannot use this tool — they must apply by [fax](/ein-by-fax/) or through [ein.so](/apply/) ($49).</Capsule>
</Section>

## How It <Accent>Works</Accent>

2. Select entity type
3. Enter [responsible party](/responsible-party-ein/) info (name + SSN/ITIN)
4. Enter business details
5. Review and submit
6. Receive EIN instantly
7. Download CP 575 confirmation
