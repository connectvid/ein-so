## IRS Phone Numbers <Accent>for EIN</Accent>

| Line | Number | For | Toll-Free? |
|------|--------|-----|------------|
| International | **267-941-1099** | Non-US residents | No |
| Domestic | **800-829-4933** | US residents | Yes |
| TTY/TDD | 800-829-4059 | Hearing impaired | Yes |

**Hours:** Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time

### Best Times to Call
- Lowest wait: Tuesday-Thursday 7-8 AM ET
- Moderate: Weekday afternoons 3-5 PM ET
- Longest: Monday mornings, Fridays, tax season

## How to Apply <Accent>by Phone</Accent>

1. **Prepare** — Fill out Form SS-4 on paper for reference
2. **Call** — 267-941-1099 (international) or 800-829-4933
3. **Navigate prompts** — Select EIN option
4. **Wait on hold** — 30-60+ minutes typical
5. **Answer questions** — IRS rep asks SS-4 questions verbally
6. **Receive EIN** — Issued during the call if approved

### Pros and Cons

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Same-day EIN | 30-60+ min hold |
| Free | Int'l calling costs |
| Ask questions live | Language barriers |
| No fax needed | Business hours only |
