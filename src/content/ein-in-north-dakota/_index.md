### Federal EIN vs North Dakota State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and North Dakota state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **North Dakota State Tax ID** — From Office of State Tax Commissioner, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
