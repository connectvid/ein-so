<Capsule>**Filing a BOI report directly with FinCEN is completely free ($0). There is no government fee.** Third-party BOI filing services charge $49 to $199 for assistance. The FinCEN e-filing system at fincen.gov/boi is straightforward and takes 15-30 minutes per company.</Capsule>

## BOI Filing Cost: <Accent>$0 (Free)</Accent>

| Method | Cost |
|--------|------|
| FinCEN direct | $0 (free) |
| Third-party services | $49-199 |
| ein.so BOI service | $49 |

### Why It's Free
FinCEN does not charge for BOI report submissions. The e-filing system is funded by the federal government. There is no filing fee, processing fee, or annual fee.

### When to Use a Service
- Multiple companies to file
- Complex ownership structures
- Non-English speaking owners
- Want hands-off filing
