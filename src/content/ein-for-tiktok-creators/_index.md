## How Do Non-US TikTok Creators Apply for an <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

Non-US TikTok creators apply by submitting IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, entering a passport number on Line 7b in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool is blocked without an SSN. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Register an LLC in a state such as Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives your creator business a US legal entity and a US address. You can form the LLC entirely online from outside the United States.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC name and formation state, and your home address. No SSN, ITIN, or US visit is required for the EIN application.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your home address on Line 4, your full legal name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. Mark "Started new business" on Line 10. ein.so completes this to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by email through ein.so.</Step>
</Steps>

### Creator SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Creator Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your home address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "content creation," "advertising," "e-commerce" |

<NumberedCards>
<Card title="W-9 for US Creators" index={1}>US creators complete Form W-9 for TikTok and every brand. You enter your EIN on the W-9, not your SSN. This single substitution keeps your Social Security Number out of every payer's records while still meeting IRS reporting rules.</Card>
<Card title="1099-NEC at $600" index={2}>TikTok and brands report payments of $600 or more on Form 1099-NEC. The form goes to you and the IRS. You report this income on your tax return. Keep records of every payout to reconcile against the 1099s you receive.</Card>

## What Must Foreign-Owned Creator LLCs <Accent>File Each Year?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member US LLC must file Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year, and most LLCs must file a BOI report with FinCEN. The Form 5472 penalty for non-filing is $25,000. These are compliance steps, separate from your EIN application.

<Alert type="warning" title="Annual obligations for foreign-owned creator LLCs">
<ol>
<li>File [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) plus a pro-forma Form 1120 every year. The non-filing penalty is $25,000.</li>
<li>File your [BOI report](/boi-filing/) with FinCEN reporting beneficial ownership of the LLC.</li>
<li>File your state annual report and pay any state franchise or filing fee. Confirm the amount with your registered agent, as state fees vary.</li>
<li>Confirm your US and home-country tax filings with a US CPA. ein.so does not give tax advice.</li>
</ol>
</Alert>

The EIN itself does not create a tax bill. It is the federal tax ID the IRS uses to track your LLC. Form 5472 reports transactions between you and your LLC, such as capital contributions and distributions. A foreign-owned single-member LLC is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax. Confirm your treatment and any treaty position with a CPA. Review the [BOI filing rules](/boi-filing/) and [EIN for non-residents](/ein-for-non-residents/) for the complete compliance picture.
