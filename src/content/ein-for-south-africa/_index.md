## How Do South African Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

South African citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a South African passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most South African founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from South Africa.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your South African passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your South African address. No Tax Reference Number or SARS registration is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your South African address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your South African passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent errors.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

### South Africa-Specific SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | South African Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your South African address (street, postal code, province) |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your South African passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "software," "e-commerce," "consulting" |

The 4-digit postal code and province do not map cleanly to the SS-4 layout, and the IRS rejects malformed international addresses without explanation. ein.so reformats South African addresses to an IRS-accepted style.
