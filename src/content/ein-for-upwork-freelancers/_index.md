## Do Upwork Freelancers <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

Upwork freelancers with a US LLC, a corporation, or employees need an EIN. Sole proprietors can use an SSN, but an EIN protects that SSN on tax forms and invoices. Non-US freelancers operating a US LLC need an EIN to complete the Upwork tax interview and open banking.

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Upwork freelancer with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Upwork freelancer with a corporation | Yes (required) |
| Upwork freelancer hiring employees | Yes (required) |
| Non-US freelancer with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| US sole proprietor, no LLC | Recommended |
| Hobby or one-off side income | No |

### Why Upwork Freelancers Get an EIN

1. **SSN privacy** — Put an EIN on W-9 forms instead of your Social Security Number.
2. **Business banking** — Open a [US business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/) for payouts.
3. **Tax separation** — Keep freelance income separate from personal tax identity.
4. **Credibility** — Present a registered US business to clients and platforms.
5. **Hiring** — An EIN is required to run payroll for any employee.

## How Do Upwork Freelancers Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Upwork freelancers submit IRS Form SS-4 to get an EIN. Non-residents fax the form to 855-215-1627 using a passport number on Line 7b instead of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so non-residents use fax. Most correctly filed EINs arrive in 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Choose Your Structure">US sole proprietors can apply with just a name. Non-residents form a US LLC first, usually in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. The LLC gives you a US entity and address to attach the EIN to.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need a valid passport, your LLC name and formation state, and your home address. No SSN or ITIN is required for the EIN. No foreign business registration number is required either.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes these fields to prevent silent rejection.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by email through ein.so.</Step>
</Steps>

### Form SS-4 Field Guide for Freelancers

| SS-4 Field | Freelancer Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Mailing address) | Your home or registered address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number (non-residents) |
| Line 10 (Reason for applying) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "software," "design," "consulting" |

The most common rejection cause is malformed international address formatting on Line 4. The IRS silently rejects addresses it cannot parse. ein.so reformats your address to an IRS-accepted style. ## What Should Upwork Freelancers Know About <Accent>EIN Taxes?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member US LLC is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax, meaning the LLC pays no US federal income tax on non-US-sourced income. It must still file Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000.

<Card title="Upwork Tax Forms" index={2}>US persons complete a W-9 and may receive a 1099-K or 1099-NEC at the platform threshold. Non-residents complete a W-8BEN-E and generally receive no 1099. Confirm your forms with a CPA.</Card>
<Card title="ITIN for Personal Filings" index={3}>Some freelancers need a personal tax ID for treaty claims or personal returns. ein.so files ITIN applications at $197 Standard and $297 Express. The EIN covers the business; the ITIN covers the individual.</Card>
