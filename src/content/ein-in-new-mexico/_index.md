### Federal EIN vs New Mexico State Tax ID

Your federal EIN and New Mexico state tax registration are separate:
- **Federal EIN** — From IRS, for federal taxes (Form SS-4)
- **New Mexico State Tax ID** — From Taxation and Revenue Department, for state taxes

You may need both. Your EIN does not replace state tax registration.
