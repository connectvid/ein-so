<Capsule>**Foreign nationals can get a US EIN by submitting IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627. No SSN or ITIN is required.** Use your passport number as your identifying number on Line 7b of Form SS-4. The IRS processes fax applications within 4-7 business days. ein.so handles the process for $49 Standard or $97 Express.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>Process</Label>
## How Foreign Nationals <Accent>Get an EIN</Accent>

1. **Form a US entity** — Register an LLC or corporation with a US state
2. **Complete [Form SS-4](/ss4-form-guide/)** — Use passport number on Line 7b
3. **Fax to [855-215-1627](/irs-fax-number-for-ein/)** — Or use ein.so ($49)
4. **Receive EIN** — 4-7 business days by fax or email

### Key Fields for Foreign Nationals

| SS-4 Line | What to Enter |
|-----------|---------------|
| 7a (Responsible party) | Your full legal name |
| 7b (SSN/ITIN) | Passport number |
| 4a-4b (Address) | Your foreign address |
| 10 (Reason) | Started new business |

For detailed instructions, see [foreign EIN application](/foreign-ein-application/).

## Why Foreign Nationals <Accent>Need an EIN</Accent>

- Open a [US business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
- Accept payments through PayPal, Stripe, Amazon
- File US tax returns
- Comply with [BOI filing](/boi-filing/) requirements
- File [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) (required for foreign-owned LLCs)
- Hire US employees or contractors
