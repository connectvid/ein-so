## How Does the ein.so EIN <Accent>Process Work?</Accent>

ein.so completes Form SS-4, faxes it to the IRS at 855-215-1627, and emails your EIN once the IRS assigns it. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days for $49. Express is 2-3 business days for $97. You provide your entity details and passport; ein.so handles the filing.

<Steps>

<Step step="2" title="ein.so Prepares Form SS-4">ein.so completes Line 1 (entity name), Line 4 (address), Line 7a (responsible party), and Line 7b (passport number), and formats your international address for the IRS. See [the SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/).</Step>

## What Happens After You Get Your <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

After approval, your EIN lets you open a US business bank account, connect US payment processors, and meet US filing duties. The EIN is the federal tax ID that links your entity to the US financial system. It is the starting point for banking, payments, and compliance, not the finish line.

<Card title="Open a US Bank Account" index={1}>Use your EIN, formation documents, and passport to open a US business account. This gives you US account and routing numbers. See [EIN for a bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/).</Card>
