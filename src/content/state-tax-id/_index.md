<Section>
<Capsule>**A state tax ID is separate from your federal EIN.** Each state issues its own tax identification numbers for state income tax, sales tax, and employer payroll tax purposes. Your federal EIN (from the IRS) does not replace state tax registrations. Most states require separate registration.</Capsule>
</Section>
