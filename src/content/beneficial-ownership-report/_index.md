<Capsule>**A Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) report discloses the individuals who own or control a company to the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN).** Required for most LLCs and corporations. Beneficial owners are individuals with 25%+ ownership or substantial control. File free at fincen.gov/boi.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Section surface>
<Label>What Is BOI</Label>

## What Is a BOI <Accent>Report?</Accent>

The Corporate Transparency Act (CTA) requires most US companies to report their beneficial owners to FinCEN. The BOI report includes:

### Company Information
- Legal name, trade names
- Address
- State of formation
- EIN or Tax ID

### Beneficial Owner Information
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Residential address
- Identifying document (passport, driver's license) + image

### Who Is a Beneficial Owner?
| Criteria | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| 25%+ ownership | Members, shareholders |
| Substantial control | CEO, CFO, President |
| Senior officers | General counsel, COO |
| Important decision makers | Anyone with authority over major decisions |
</Section>

## How to File <Accent>a BOI Report</Accent>

File at fincen.gov/boi. The process takes 15-30 minutes per company.

1. Go to fincen.gov/boi
2. Click 'File BOIR'
3. Enter company details + EIN
4. Add each beneficial owner
5. Upload ID document images
6. Submit
7. Save confirmation

The filing is **free**. See [FinCEN BOI filing](/fincen-boi-filing/) for the detailed walkthrough.
