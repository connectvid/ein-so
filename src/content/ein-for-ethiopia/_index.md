## How Do Ethiopian Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Ethiopian citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using an Ethiopia passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Ethiopian founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You complete the formation online from Ethiopia.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Ethiopia passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Ethiopia address. No TIN or Ethiopian registration document is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Ethiopia address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Ethiopia passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent errors.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575).</Step>
</Steps>

## What Should Ethiopian Entrepreneurs Know About <Accent>US and Ethiopian Taxes?</Accent>

A single-member US LLC owned by an Ethiopian resident is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax, meaning the LLC pays no US federal income tax on non-US-sourced income. You still carry annual US information-reporting duties, and you continue to report income in Ethiopia under local rules.

<NumberedCards>
<Card title="US Filing: Form 5472 + 1120" index={1}>A foreign-owned single-member US LLC must file [Form 5472](/form-5472-guide/) with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. This is an information return, not always a tax bill.</Card>
<Card title="BOI Report to FinCEN" index={2}>Most US LLCs report beneficial ownership to FinCEN. Review the [BOI filing rules](/boi-filing/) to confirm your deadline and avoid penalties. The BOI filing itself is free at fincen.gov/boi.</Card>
