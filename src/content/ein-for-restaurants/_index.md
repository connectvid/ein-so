<Capsule>**Restaurants & Food Trucks need an EIN if they operate as an LLC, corporation, or hire employees.** Food businesses need an EIN for employee hiring, health permits, and vendor accounts. The EIN is free from the IRS. Sole proprietors can use their SSN but an EIN is recommended for separating business and personal finances. ein.so handles EIN applications for $49.</Capsule>

## Do Restaurants & Food Trucks <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

|-----------|------------|
| Restaurants & Food Trucks LLC | Yes (required) |

### Why Restaurants & Food Trucks Get an EIN

1. **Tax compliance** — Required on business tax returns
2. **Business banking** — Open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
3. **Privacy** — Protect your SSN on invoices and W-9s
4. **Credibility** — Professional business identity
5. **Hiring** — Required for [employee payroll](/ein-for-hiring-employees/)
