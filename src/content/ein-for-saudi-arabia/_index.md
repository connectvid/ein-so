## How Do Saudi Citizens Apply for a <Accent>US EIN?</Accent>

Saudi citizens submit IRS Form SS-4 by fax to 855-215-1627, using a Saudi passport number in place of an SSN. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only viable path for Saudi applicants. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most Saudi founders register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This gives you a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You can form the LLC entirely online from Saudi Arabia.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your Saudi passport (valid and unexpired), your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and your Saudi address. No Saudi TIN or commercial registration is required for the EIN application.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your Saudi address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your Saudi passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this for you to prevent the errors that cause IRS rejection. See the [SS-4 form guide](/ss4-form-guide/).</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by fax or mail.</Step>
</Steps>

### Saudi Arabia-Specific SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Saudi Entry |

| Line 4 (Address) | Your Saudi address (district, city, postal code) |

## What Annual Filings Does a Saudi-Owned <Accent>US LLC Have?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member US LLC must file Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year, and the penalty for not filing is $25,000. Most US LLCs also report beneficial ownership to FinCEN. These are information returns, not automatic tax bills.
