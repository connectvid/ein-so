<Capsule>**Form W-7 (Application for IRS Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) is the official form to apply for an ITIN.** Submit Form W-7 with a federal tax return and identity documents to the IRS. The form has 6 sections covering personal information, reason for applying, and supporting documents.</Capsule>
</Section>

<Label>Overview</Label>
## Form W-7 <Accent>Overview</Accent>

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| Full name | Application for IRS Individual Taxpayer Identification Number |
| Current version | Rev. August 2019 |
| Pages | 4 (form) + 12 (instructions) |
| Filing method | Mail, CAA, or in-person |
| Must include | Federal tax return + ID documents |

Download from irs.gov. See [how to get an ITIN](/how-to-get-itin/) for the complete process.
</Section>

<Section>
<Label>Key Sections</Label>
## Key Form W-7 <Accent>Sections</Accent>

### Section 1: Reason for Submitting
- Box a: New ITIN (nonresident alien filing tax return)
- Box b: New ITIN (claimed as dependent)
- Box d: New ITIN (student)
- Box h: Renewal

### Section 2: Personal Information
- Name, address, date of birth, country of citizenship, foreign tax ID

### Section 3: Documents
- Passport (standalone) OR 2 alternative documents
- Original or certified copies only
</Section>

<Section wide>
<Label>Tips</Label>
## Filing Tips

1. Use your **legal name** exactly as it appears on your passport
2. **Reason code** must match your situation exactly
3. Include **original documents** (not photocopies) unless using a CAA
4. Attach your **federal tax return** (Form 1040 or 1040-NR)
