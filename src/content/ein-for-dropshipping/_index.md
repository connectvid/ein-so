## Does a Dropshipping Business <Accent>Need an EIN?</Accent>

A dropshipping business needs an EIN if it operates as an LLC, operates as a corporation, or hires employees. A sole proprietor with no employees can use an SSN, but suppliers and processors usually request an EIN. An EIN keeps your personal Social Security number off business records.

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dropshipping US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Dropshipping corporation | Yes (required) |
| Dropshipping sole proprietor, no employees | Recommended |
| Dropshipping with employees | Yes (required) |
| Non-US resident with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Hobby reselling with no business setup | No |

### Why a Dropshipping Business Gets an EIN

1. **Sales tax registration** — US states require an EIN to issue a sales tax permit
2. **Payment processing** — Stripe and Shopify Payments request a US tax ID for a US entity
3. **Supplier accounts** — Wholesalers ask for an EIN to open a reseller account
4. **Business banking** — Banks require an EIN to open a [business bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/)
5. **Privacy** — The EIN protects your SSN on W-9s and invoices

A non-US resident with a US LLC always needs an EIN. The LLC cannot open a bank account, enroll with Stripe, or file US taxes without one. See [who needs an EIN](/ein-for-non-residents/) for full eligibility details.

## How Does a Non-US Resident Apply for a <Accent>Dropshipping EIN?</Accent>

A non-US resident applies for a dropshipping EIN by faxing IRS Form SS-4 to 855-215-1627 with a passport number on Line 7b. The IRS online tool requires an SSN, so the fax method is the only path for non-residents. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most non-US dropshippers register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. The LLC gives you a US business entity and address to attach the EIN to. You complete this entirely online.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need a valid passport, your LLC name, your LLC formation state, your formation date, and your home address. No SSN, no ITIN, and no US visit are required.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes the form to prevent rejection.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter, the CP 575.</Step>
</Steps>

### SS-4 Field Guide for Dropshippers

| SS-4 Field | Dropshipping Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your home or registered agent address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | "Retail" or "e-commerce" |

The most common rejection cause is address formatting that does not map to the SS-4 layout. The IRS silently rejects malformed international addresses, which adds weeks. ein.so reformats your address to an IRS-accepted style. ## What Taxes Does a Dropshipping <Accent>LLC Owe?</Accent>

A single-member US LLC owned by a non-US resident is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax. The LLC itself pays no US federal income tax on non-US-sourced income. A foreign-owned single-member LLC still files Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. Confirm your situation with a US CPA.

## Does a Dropshipper Need an EIN or an <Accent>ITIN?</Accent>

A dropshipping business needs an EIN, which is the federal tax ID for the business entity. An ITIN is a personal tax ID for an individual who must file a US personal return. The two serve different purposes, and a dropshipping LLC starts with the EIN.

| ID | Who It Is For | When a Dropshipper Needs It | ein.so Price |
|----|---------------|-----------------------------|--------------|
| EIN | The business entity | To run the LLC, bank, and pay suppliers | $49 / $97 |
| ITIN | An individual | If you must file a US personal tax return | $197 / $297 |

Most non-US dropshippers need only the EIN. An ITIN becomes relevant when an individual has a US personal filing obligation, which a US CPA confirms. The ein.so ITIN service costs $197 (Standard) or $297 (Express). Read more on the [EIN without SSN](/ein-without-ssn/) page.
