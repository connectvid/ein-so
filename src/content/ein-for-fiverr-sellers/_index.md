## Do Fiverr Sellers Need an <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

Fiverr sellers who operate through a US LLC, corporation, or who hire employees need an EIN. Sole proprietors can use an SSN, but an EIN keeps that number private and enables business banking. The EIN is the federal tax ID for the business.

A Fiverr seller crosses the threshold for an EIN at the moment they form a legal entity or want to keep business funds separate. The table below maps common Fiverr situations to the EIN requirement.

| Situation | EIN Needed? |
| --- | --- |
| Fiverr seller with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Fiverr seller as US sole proprietor | Recommended |
| Fiverr seller with employees or contractors on payroll | Yes (required) |
| Non-US Fiverr seller with a US LLC | Yes (required) |
| Hobby or one-off gig income only | No |

### Why Fiverr Sellers Get an EIN

1. **Tax compliance** — The EIN appears on business returns and W-9 forms.
2. **Business banking** — A US business bank account requires an EIN to open. See [EIN for a bank account](/ein-for-bank-account/).
3. **Privacy** — The EIN replaces the SSN on invoices, contracts, and W-9s.
4. **Credibility** — A US business identity reassures higher-value clients.
5. **Eligibility** — See [who needs an EIN](/who-needs-ein/) for the complete IRS criteria.

## How Do Fiverr Sellers Apply for an <Accent>EIN?</Accent>

Fiverr sellers apply for an EIN by submitting IRS Form SS-4. Non-residents fax the form to 855-215-1627 using a passport number on Line 7b in place of an SSN. The IRS issues most correctly filed EINs within 4-7 business days. The IRS online tool requires an SSN.

<Steps>
<Step step="1" title="Form Your US LLC">Most non-US Fiverr sellers register an LLC in Wyoming, Delaware, or New Mexico through a registered agent. This creates a US business address and a legal entity to attach the EIN to. You complete the formation entirely online.</Step>
<Step step="2" title="Gather Your Documents">You need your valid passport, your LLC formation details (name, state, formation date), and a mailing address. No SSN, ITIN, or local business registration is required for the EIN.</Step>
<Step step="3" title="Complete Form SS-4">Enter your US LLC name on Line 1, your address on Line 4, your full legal name as responsible party on Line 7a, and your passport number on Line 7b. ein.so completes this to prevent rejections.</Step>
<Step step="4" title="Submit by Fax and Receive Your EIN">The SS-4 is faxed to the IRS at 855-215-1627. Standard delivery is 4-7 business days; Express is 2-3 business days. The IRS returns your EIN assignment letter (CP 575) by email through ein.so.</Step>
</Steps>

### Fiverr Seller SS-4 Field Guide

| SS-4 Field | Fiverr Seller Entry |
| --- | --- |
| Line 1 (Legal name) | Your US LLC name |
| Line 4 (Address) | Your mailing or business address |
| Line 7a (Responsible party) | Your full name as on your passport |
| Line 7b (SSN/ITIN/EIN) | Your passport number (non-residents) |
| Line 10 (Reason) | Started new business |
| Line 16 (Principal activity) | E.g., "freelance services," "design," "software" |

## What Should Fiverr Sellers Know About <Accent>US Taxes?</Accent>

A foreign-owned single-member US LLC is usually a disregarded entity for US federal income tax. It files Form 5472 with a pro-forma Form 1120 each year. The penalty for not filing is $25,000. This is an information return, not automatically a tax bill.
