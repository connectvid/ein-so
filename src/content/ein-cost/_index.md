## IRS EIN Application: <Accent>$0 (Free)</Accent>

The IRS has never charged a fee for EIN applications. Every method is free:

| Method | Cost | Processing Time | Requirements |
|--------|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Online (EIN Assistant) | $0 | Instant | SSN or ITIN |
| Fax (Form SS-4) | $0 | 4-7 business days | None |
| Phone (267-941-1099) | $0 | Same day | None |
| Mail (Form SS-4) | $0 | 4-6 weeks | None |

The EIN is free regardless of your business type (LLC, corporation, sole proprietorship, trust, estate, nonprofit), your location (US or international), or the number of EINs you have applied for previously.
